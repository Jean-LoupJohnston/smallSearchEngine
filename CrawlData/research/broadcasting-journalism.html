Engaging media through critical research & archival study

The Centre is the legal depository for the CBC Radio Drama Archive (1925 - present) and the Diniacopoulos BBC World News Collection (1969 - 86). Our research focuses on multidisciplinary analyses of media, journalism, and society.

Read about our 2017-2018 Conference Series: Truth and Democracy: Journalism, Politics, and Social Science
