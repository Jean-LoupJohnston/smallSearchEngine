Community Programs

The PERFORM Centre engages the community by offering health programs and services to the general population but also through the opportunity to participate in groundbreaking research

Athletic Therapy Clinic

PERFORM's Athletic Therapy Clinic delivers prompt and professional care to staff and faculty, students as well as the general public. The Clinic is a fully functional treatment center with the very latest in equipment and modalities, including Swim-Ex for aqua therapy.

Gym Memberships

A membership in the PERFORM Gym allows you to benefit from the latest teaching methods, the research findings, and advanced fitness equipment which allows you to track your personal goals and progress. Participants are encouraged to take part in PERFORM's research and training programs.

Facilities

PERFORM houses laboratories, conditioning equipment and functional assessment suites each dedicated to research. All its facilities were designed to foster a multidisciplinary environment in which researchers can work towards achieving better health through prevention.
