    Partnerships and innovation

    University-industry partnerships benefit everyone.  Concordia creates spaces where ideas can breathe by offering coaching and networking opportunities for next-generation visionaries.

    Partnerships allow Concordia to:

    • Share its research expertise, capacity and infrastructure,
    • Improve Quebec and Canada's innovation performance, and
    • Expose students to real-world issues and problems.
