Deadlines

Students Account Services are now located in the Birks Student Service Centre

Find out more

The University reserves the right to modify the published scale of tuition and other student fees without prior notice, at any time before the beginning of an academic term. This document contains the most current information on the University's Financial Regulations and Fees and is the authoritative document regarding the University's financial regulations as well as the billing of tuition and compulsory fees.

Academic Term Payment Deadline
(i.e. payment must be posted to the student's account no later than)		 Consequences for non-payment by the payment deadline
Summer 
courses

May - August

All summer fees  with the exception of second term summer classes1become due May 1.

Final payment deadline is May 31 after which, late payment and interest fees will be applied.

1 Second term summer fees become due as of July 1. The final payment deadline for second term summer classes is July 31, after which, late payment and interest fees will be applied. 
  • A $77.50 late payment fee will be charged;
  • monthly interest charges will be applied to overdue balances beginning June 1; and
  • go to Consequences for late payment and non-payment of fees for further information.
Fall 
courses

September - December

Fees are due September 1.

Final payment deadline is September 30 after which, late payment and interest fees will be applied.

** Any tuition & other fees incurred from a registration made after September 30th is due immediately.
  • A $77.50 late payment fee will be charged;
  • monthly interest charges will be applied to overdue balances beginning October 1; and
  • go to Consequences for late payment and non-payment of fees for further information.
Fall / Winter
courses

September - April

50% of fall/winter Fees (/3 courses) are due on September 1.

Final payment deadline is September 30 after which, late payment and interest fees will be applied.

** Any tuition & other fees incurred from a registration made after September 30th is due immediately.
  • A $77.50 late payment fee will be charged;
  • monthly interest charges will be applied to overdue balances beginning October 1;  and
  • go to Consequences for late payment and non-payment of fees for further information.
Balance of fall/winter Fees (/3 courses) are due on January 1.

Final payment deadline is January 31 after which, late payment and interest fees will be applied.

** Any tuition & other fees incurred from a registration made after January 31st is due immediately.
  • A $77.50 late payment fee will be charged;
  • monthly interest charges will be applied to overdue balances beginning February 1; and
  • go to Consequences for late payment and non-payment of fees for further information.
  • Note: The DNE deadline for /3 fall/winter courses is September 16, 2019 and NOT January 20, 2020.

Winter 
courses

January - April

Fees (/4 courses) are due January 1.

Final payment deadline is January 31 after which, late payment and interest fees will be applied.

** Any tuition & other fees incurred from a registration made after January 31st is due immediately.
  • A $77.50 late payment fee will be charged;
  • monthly interest charges will be applied to overdue balances beginning February 1; and
  • go to Consequences for late-payment and non-payment of fees for further information.

Note:
When a payment deadline falls on a Saturday, Sunday or legal holiday, payments will be accepted until 7 pm on the next business day. Payments received after that time will be subject to a late payment fee and monthly interest charges.
