Mentoring

Concordia Alumni Connect

Create a profile on Concordia's online alumni mentoring platform. Connect with others who share your Concordia experience and discover connections to further your career.

On Concordia Alumni Connect, you can:

  • Build your professional network
  • Better understand your career trajectory
  • Get support for career planning
  • Reflect on your current role and future goals
  • Find someone to turn to for questions and help
  • Find someone who can act as a potential reference for you
  • Make meaningful professional connections worldwide
  • Achieve higher job satisfaction

Find out more >>

Imposter, Interrupted

Founded by Sophie Babinski, BA 14, Imposter, Interrupted has as its mission to collect stories, build community, and share knowledge and compassion among professional women in order to close the wage gap and usher in gender parity at the executive level.

Listen to the Imposter, Interrupted podcast  >>

Mentorly

Mentorly is the first online arts mentorship platform providing accessible and engaging opportunities to connect with emerging and professional artists around the world. It was founded by professional dancer Ashley Werhun and filmmaker Katherine Macnaughton, BA 07  as working artists, they experienced a significant gap in mentorship within the arts.

After speaking to hundreds of artists and assessing their all-too-common pain points, the gap led to an urgent call to action: provide artists with direct access to mentors who are eager to help them grow and steer their careers forward.
