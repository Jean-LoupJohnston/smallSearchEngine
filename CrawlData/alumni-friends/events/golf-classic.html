CONCORDIA GOLF CLASSIC

An annual tradition since 2004

An annual tradition since 2004, the Concordia Golf Classic is one of the university's most successful fundraisers.

Thanks to our generous sponsors, we've raised nearly $5.4 million for bursaries and scholarships and have helped more than 1,500 promising students. Yet the need continues  over half of Concordia"s 50,000 students rely on financial aid for books, tuition fees and living expenses. 

Join us for a beautiful day on the green  or as a sponsor and benefit from visibility at our highly successful event.

Monday, September 9, 2019

Golf Griffon des Sources, Mirabel, Quebec

Schedule:
9 a.m. Registration
9 a.m.11 a.m. Brunch and driving range
11 a.m. Shotgun start (individual play format)
5:30 p.m. Cocktail
6:30 p.m. Dinner and door prizes
8:45 p.m. Raffle
9 p.m. Conclusion

Cost: $5,000 per foursome | $ 1,250 per player | Limit: 32 foursomes

Gain visibility for your brand and support an important cause.

As a sponsor, your organization really can change lives.
