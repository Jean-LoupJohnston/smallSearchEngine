The Loyola Sustainability Research Centre

The Loyola Sustainability Research Centre aims to make a lasting contribution to the transdisciplinary dialogue that is shaping our collective understanding of sustainable futures.

At the Loyola Sustainability Research Centre, over three dozen Concordia researchers from all four faculties, 20 external collaborators, and more than a dozen graduate students work together to create a constructive intellectual space, pursue team grants and conduct integrated research, and engage in community outreach activities.
