Student Success Resource Centre

 Career Resource Centre

The Career Resource Centre (CRC), is located in room H-757, Henry F. Hall Building on the SGW Campus. The drop-in centre provides print, electronic and audio-visual resources to help students in their career exploration. We also have an extensive collection of leadership, educational planning, career planning and job search resources. Our dedicated staff will help you find the information you need. They collect a wide range of materials to help you achieve your utmost when it comes to your personal growth and development. In addition, computer workstations in the Career Resource Centre are available to students for accessing the Internet. We also have a study space accessible for students. All students are welcome!
