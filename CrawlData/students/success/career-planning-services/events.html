Career Panels

Learn more about career options in your major, or within your field of interest by attending an exciting series of panel discussions. Concordia graduates will share their career paths and provide insight into how to achieve career success.

Borrow and watch DVD archives of past career panels in your field by dropping by the Student Success Resource Centre located on the SGW Campus in room H-757.

Open to all interested Concordia students and alumni.
