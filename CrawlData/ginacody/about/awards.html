Award for Teaching Excellence

The Award for Teaching Excellence is given to motivate, recognize and reward excellence in teaching in the following categories:

  • Sustained excellence in teaching
  • Leadership and innovation in teaching
  • Excellence in teaching by a Junior Faculty Member
  • Excellence in teaching tutorials
  • Excellence in laboratory instruction

Two Staff Recognition Awards will be presented in each academic year* depending on the caliber of the nominations.
