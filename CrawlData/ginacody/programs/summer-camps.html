SUMMER CAMPS

Science and Engineering Summer Camp

For kids ages 6-12

This program lets campers explore the worlds of science, technology, mathematics and engineering. With a new theme each week, they'll experience interactive workshops, play outside, and go on a field trip â¬ to Montreal institutions such as the Canadian Space Agency and the Planetarium.

All-Girls Summer Engineering and Technology program

For girls in grades 8-11 and CEGEP

Careers in engineering and technology are all about creativity, innovation and leadership. This free two-week program gives young women hands-on engineering experience through design challenges, interactive labs and informative activities.
