John Molson Executive MBA

Ranked #2 in Canada by The Economist. Read more.

Why the John Molson Executive MBA?

20-MONTH PROGRAM

Includes 15 months of class time, summer and winter breaks and a study trip

CONVENIENT SCHEDULE

Classes on alternating Fridays and Saturdays make it family- and work-friendly

GLOBAL EXPERTISE

Professors who work in their fields bring real-world knowledge

HANDS-ON CURRICULUM

Courses and group projects include contact with industry and top-level executives

SMALL COHORT

Limited class size allows for one-on-one engagement with faculty

EXECUTIVE FACILITIES

Dedicated space for learning, meeting and dining devoted to the EMBA program

DIVERSITY

Each EMBA cohort makes for a rich and engaging experience for participants

SUPPORTING MODULES

Class champions, coaching and mentors support your progress throughout

EMBA provides mutual benefit

For the employee

Experienced managers further develop their careers by deepening knowledge and acquiring new skills. 

For the employer

Organizations benefit from new expertise from a valued employee without incurring any downtime. 

Consider our EMBA for its return on investment
