  • Bravemind

    Bravemind helps treat Veterans who suffer from Post-traumatic Stress (PTSD). Learn more.

  • VITA4VETS

    The Virtual Interactive Training Agent for Veterans ( VITA4Vets) is a virtual simulation practice system designed to build job interviewing competence and confidence, while reducing anxiety in military service members and Veterans. Learn more.

  • One World Terrain (OWT)

    One World Terrain (OWT) is an applied research project designed to assist the DoD in creating the most realistic, accurate and informative representations of the physical and non-physical landscape. Learn more.

  • Rapid Avatar Generator

    In a matter of minutes, this application creates a digital model of a person, places this 3D avatar into a video game or simulation and infuses it with behaviors and capabilities. Learn more.

ABOUT

At the University of Southern California Institute for Creative Technologies (ICT), leaders in the artificial intelligence, graphics, virtual reality and narrative communities are working to advance immersive techniques and technologies to solve problems facing service members, students and society.
RESEARCH

From creating believable virtual humans to understanding how they can best be used in the real world, research at ICT aims to literally change the face of computing.
PROTOTYPES

Using science and storytelling, ICT prototypes provide engaging experiences to improve skills in decision-making, cultural awareness, leadership and coping, to name a few.
ACADEMICS

The Institute for Creative Technologies offers academic opportunities in a multidisciplinary, creative environment that encourages collaboration across a number of fields.
