About Us

Visit Bellevue serves as the official Destination Marketing & Management Organization (DMMO) for Bellevue, Washington -- representing the city and region's hotels, attractions, restaurants, retailers, events and convention center. We are the collective voice for the entire Bellevue hospitality and tourism community.

2.2 million annual visitors to Bellevue
$81.6 million in sales tax revenue
$1.76 billion in economic impact for our region

Through the impact of travel, and by increasing the number of visitors, we strengthen Bellevue's economic position, bringing direct economic benefit to our city and providing an elevated quality of life for our community.

We are governed and managed under the Bellevue Convention Center Authority (BCCA) and guided by an advisory board comprised of civic and business leaders.

Our dedicated and passionate staff is eager to engage with you. We are excited to provide information, resources and inspiration for a memorable experience in Bellevue and the greater Puget Sound region.

For specific questions about bringing a convention, meeting or event to Bellevue, contact our experienced sales team, or to partner with Visit Bellevue, reach out to our partnership manager. Or for general inquiries, please call us by phone at 877-425-2075 or connect with us by email.
